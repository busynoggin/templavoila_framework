================================
Utility FCEs: Providing Layout Options
================================

Across the web, sites have a variety of ways of arranging content within content areas. This framework provides Utility FCEs that handle some of the more common ways of laying out content, in particular, columns and modules.

Of course, a developer still has all the templating power of TemplaVoila at his fingertips to construct FCEs that handle specialized layout needs.
